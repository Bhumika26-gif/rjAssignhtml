There is no doubt as to who is at number 1 and who is the greatest Indian cricketer of all time. It has to be none other than Sachin Tendulkar. He has played the most Test Matches at 200 and the most ODI Matches at 463. He has made over 34000 international runs and he has made 100 international Hundreds in international cricket. He has served Indian cricket for 24 years and he is the first cricketer to be awarded the Bharat Ratna. Sachin Tendulkar is the benchmark for Indian cricket. He has won the ODI World Cup in 2011.
