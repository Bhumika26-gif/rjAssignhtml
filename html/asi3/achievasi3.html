In recognition of its efforts to penetrate globally, HALDIRAM’S bagged the prestigious ‘INTERNATIONAL AWARD FOR FOOD & BEVERAGES’ awarded by Trade Leaders Club in Barcelona, Italy, Spain in the year 1994.
HALDIRAM’S is honoured to be a member of Snacks Food Association (SFA), Virginia, U.S.A. which comprises of world renowned food product manufacturers.
The Group has also to its credit 'KASHALKAR MEMORIAL AWARD' presented by 'All India Food Preservers Association' (Regd.) in 1996 at its Golden Jubilee Celebration for manufacturing best quality food products. 'BRAND EQUITY AWARD 1998' was awarded by Progress Harmony Development Chamber of Commerce & Industry through Shri Yashwant Sinha, former Union Finance Minister recognizing the successful creation of Indian Brand 'HALDIRAM'S'. Haldiram’s has also been awarded with APEDA EXPORT AWARD for its highest quality products.
